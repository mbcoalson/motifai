1. **Physical Description**: 16 years old, 5'7", distinguishing features: short dark hair, large brown eyes, pale skin, pretty. 
2. **Personality Traits**: Fashion-conscious and presentation-focused. Very self-assured in her own intelligence. She knows she should be nice to people, but she struggles dealing with people who she sees as less intelligent.   
3. **Background**: She's been raised by her Grandmother (Grandmother's name?) in Naples, FL.  Her [[Mother]], a social awkward, likely autistic, Computer Scientist works at NASA's as the head of Artificial Intelligence for unmanned space missions. Her parents were both software engineers. She never met her father. To the best of what Anais has been able to figure out, he was a one night stand that her mother simply never called back.  
4. **Motivations**: Anais is fascinated with technology and the application of AI and works closely with the Anne system her mother has developed for her. 
5. **Relationships**: Anais's primary relationships are with [[Anne]] and her Grandmother. 
6. **Role in the Story**: Anais is a central character to this story. Her disdain for what she believes is dumb, or pointless, will drive much of the conflict of the story. The climax of the story will be when Anais stands up to [[Joseph Krutz]] and his fundamental religious tribe.
